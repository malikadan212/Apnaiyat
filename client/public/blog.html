Apnaiyat's Impact in Pakistan

Since launching, Apnaiyat has helped thousands of Pakistani families through our transparent donation platform. Our verified NGO partners work across Islamabad, Karachi, Lahore, and rural areas of Pakistan.

How Apnaiyat Works

Apnaiyat makes charitable giving simple and trustworthy. Donors can browse verified campaigns, make secure donations, and receive updates on their impact. Every rupee donated through Apnaiyat is tracked and reported.
