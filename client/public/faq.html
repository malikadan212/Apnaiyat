Apnaiyat FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is Apnaiyat?

Apnaiyat is Pakistan's leading transparent charity platform that connects verified donors with legitimate NGOs and families in need. Founded by Adan Malik and Dure Shahwar, Apnaiyat ensures 100% transparency in charitable giving across Pakistan.

How does Apnaiyat verify campaigns?

Apnaiyat has a rigorous verification process where our team personally visits families, verifies documents, and conducts background checks. Every campaign on Apnaiyat is thoroughly vetted before going live.

Does Apnaiyat charge any fees?

No, Apnaiyat charges zero platform fees. 100% of your donation goes directly to the beneficiary. Apnaiyat is committed to maximizing the impact of every rupee donated.

Is Apnaiyat safe and secure?

Yes, Apnaiyat uses bank-level security for all transactions. Your personal and payment information is encrypted and protected. Apnaiyat is trusted by thousands of Pakistani donors.

How can I track my donation on Apnaiyat?

Apnaiyat provides complete transparency. After donating, you'll receive updates on how your money was used, including photos and reports from the beneficiary family or NGO.

Can NGOs join Apnaiyat?

Yes, registered Pakistani NGOs can apply to join Apnaiyat. We have a thorough vetting process to ensure only legitimate organizations are part of the Apnaiyat platform.

How do I contact Apnaiyat support?

You can reach Apnaiyat support at contact@apnaiyatpk.com or call +92 300 0329373. Our team is available 24/7 to help with any questions about the Apnaiyat platform.

What areas does Apnaiyat serve?

Apnaiyat serves all of Pakistan, including major cities like Islamabad, Karachi, Lahore, Peshawar, and rural areas. Our network of verified partners ensures Apnaiyat can help families nationwide.

How is Apnaiyat different from other charity platforms?

Apnaiyat is unique because of our 100% verification rate, zero fees, complete transparency, and focus on the Pakistani market. Unlike other platforms, Apnaiyat personally verifies every single campaign.

Can I donate to Apnaiyat from outside Pakistan?

Yes, Apnaiyat accepts international donations. Pakistani expatriates and international donors can contribute to verified campaigns through the Apnaiyat platform.
