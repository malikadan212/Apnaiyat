Apnaiyat Press Kit

About Apnaiyat

Apnaiyat is Pakistan's leading transparent charity platform, founded in 2024 by Adan Malik (CTO) and Dure Shahwar (CMO). Based in Islamabad, Apnaiyat connects verified donors with legitimate NGOs and families in need across Pakistan.

Company Facts

  • Founded: 2024
  • Headquarters: Islamabad, Pakistan
  • Website: apnaiyatpk.com
  • Mission: Making charitable giving transparent and trustworthy in Pakistan
  • Platform Fees: 0% - All donations go to beneficiaries
  • Verification Rate: 100% of campaigns verified

Founder Information

Adan Malik - Co-Founder & CTO

Adan Malik is the technical visionary behind Apnaiyat, passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems in Pakistan's charitable sector.

Dure Shahwar - Co-Founder & CMO

Dure Shahwar leads marketing and community building at Apnaiyat, focused on creating meaningful connections between donors and causes across Pakistan.
